# Using Fill

Fill turns your screen into a light or a solid color. Tap or click anywhere to
show the controls; they hide again after a few seconds.

## The controls
- **Swatches** — Black, White (a light you can tune), Red, or **+** to type any
  color (e.g. `#ff8800`, `tomato`, `rgb(20 20 20)`).
- **Brightness** — how bright/dim the screen is.
- **Temperature** — only for White: slide warm (cozy amber) to cool (pure white).

## Quick ways to adjust
- **Drag up/down** anywhere → brightness.
- **Drag left/right** (when on White) → warmth.
- **Arrow keys** → `↑/↓` brightness, `←/→` warmth. Letters: `B` `W` `R` `C`.
- **`F`** fullscreen.

## Lock it (lightbox / tracing)
Press **`L`** (or pick *Lock screen* in the `?` menu). The screen stays on and
ignores touches — great for cleaning it, tracing on a tablet, or as a steady
light. **Hold your finger down for about a second to unlock** (wiping won't
unlock it). `Esc` works too.

## Share & remember
Your setup is saved and reopens as you left it. The web address updates to match,
so you can bookmark or share an exact look — e.g. `fill.tavlean.com/#ff8800`.

## Handy uses
- **Video-call light** — White, tune brightness + warmth.
- **Lightbox / tracing** — White, full brightness, Lock.
- **Night light** — White, warm, low brightness.
- **Bias light** — a dim color behind your TV/monitor.
- **Dead-pixel check / screen cleaning** — Black, White, or Red full screen.
